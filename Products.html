DTF Printing

DTF Printing is a high quality printing for t-shirts where a speacial film is trasnfered by heat onto a t-shirt.

Sublimation Printing

Sublimation printing is a printing process where heat is used to transfer dye onto materials like polyester fabric, ceramic, metal, or specially coated surfaces.
It's known for producing vibrant, long-lasting, and high-quality prints that become part of the material itself, rather than sitting on top like in vinyl or screen printing.

What It can work on:
Mugs

Plates

Phone cases

Keychains

Mousepads

T-shirts

Glass Cutting Boards
